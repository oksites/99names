786 As-Sabur 99
Anyone who repeats this Name 100 times before sunrise, will be safeguarded against all calamities for the remainder of the day.

Moreover, Allah will cause his enemies not to utter a single word against him.

Any person in difficulty should repeat this Ism 1,020 times: he will soon find relief & be granted peace & contentment of heart.
🤲 Insha Allah 🤲
